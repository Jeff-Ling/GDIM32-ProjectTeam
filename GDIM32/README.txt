Press "Enter" to shoot & attack.

Press "↑", "↓" to move. 

Press "←", "→" to adjust the field of view (FOV).

Victory condition: Kill the gangster.

Failure condition: Be shot at least four times by the gangster / HP empty.

* Do NOT attempt to take down the gangster from his front, he is very DANGEROUS!
